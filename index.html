OUR CRAFT

THE WORK

Every bottle tells a story. Every story begins with intention.

01

Sourcing Ingredients

We travel to the most remote corners of the world — Grasse, Madagascar, the Oud forests of Southeast Asia — to handpick ingredients of uncompromising purity. Only the finest raw materials make their way into our atelier.

02

The Blending Atelier

Our master perfumers spend months, sometimes years, layering top notes, heart notes, and base notes until the composition breathes as one. Each formula is hand-written and sealed — a secret passed down through generations.

03

Maceration & Aging

After blending, every fragrance rests in cool darkness for a minimum of six weeks. This maceration period allows each note to marry, deepen, and fully express its character before a single drop is bottled.

04

The Final Bottle

Each flacon is hand-filled, sealed with a hand-tied ribbon, and numbered. The bottle itself is a sculpture — designed to sit on your vanity as a piece of art long after the last drop is gone.

WHERE IT BEGAN

A single bottle.
A lifelong pursuit.

It started in a small apartment in Grasse, France — the perfume capital of the world — where our founder, surrounded by bottles of raw oud, jasmine absolute, and Himalayan cedarwood, blended the first formula by hand on a wooden table that still sits in our atelier today.

What began as a personal obsession became a quiet revolution. Word spread not through advertising, but through memory — through the moment someone wearing our fragrance walked into a room and made everyone stop and ask: "What is that scent?"

"

I didn't want to make a perfume. I wanted to make a feeling you could wear.

— Founder, 1998

MILESTONES

Twenty-six years
of quiet obsession

1998

The First Blend

Our founder hand-blends the first formula in a Grasse apartment. Twenty bottles are made. All twenty are gifted to friends.

2003

The Atelier Opens

A small studio in Paris opens its doors by appointment only. The waiting list grows to six months within the first year.

2009

First International Harvest

James Osei joins and takes the first sourcing trip to Madagascar, establishing our direct-harvest model that every ingredient now follows.

2015

The Numbered Edition

We introduce hand-numbered bottles and provenance cards. The practice, radical at the time, becomes our defining signature.

2024

26 Years, 38 Fragrances

We have released thirty-eight fragrances in twenty-six years. Every one is still in production. None have ever been discontinued.
